# TaxProperty V2.1 — App Store Screenshots

Visual design system: Slate-900 (#0f172a) primary, Emerald-500 (#10b981) accent, score colors (emerald/blue/amber/red), white rounded-xl cards, clean sans-serif typography.

---

## Screenshot 1: Landing Page

**Title:** Tax Sale Intelligence for Property Investors

**Visual Description:**
Dark-themed landing page with large hero text reading "Tax Sale Intelligence for Serious Investors" in white against the slate-900 background. Below the headline sits a stats banner displaying "127 Properties | Utah County | Sale Date: May 21 2026" in a compact white card with emerald accent border. Three feature icons are arranged horizontally: ShieldCheck (representing data verification), Layers (representing multi-layered analysis), and FileCheck (representing due diligence documentation). The composition draws the eye downward toward a primary CTA button.

**Caption:** "Discover tax sale opportunities"

---

## Screenshot 2: Properties List

**Title:** Smart Property Scoring and Ranking

**Visual Description:**
Dense data table layout showing multiple property rows with thumbnail images on the left side of each entry. Each row displays color-coded score badges—emerald for strong scores, amber for fair, red for poor—positioned prominently before property addresses. Recommendation pills read BID, RESEARCH, or AVOID in filled caps. A filter bar sits above the table with dropdown selectors for county, sale date, and score range. Bulk action buttons appear in the top right for selecting multiple properties for export or comparison.

**Caption:** "127 scored and ranked properties"

---

## Screenshot 3: Property Detail

**Title:** Complete Property Due Diligence View

**Visual Description:**
Full-screen property detail view with a Street View panoramic image serving as the header banner. Below, three score cards are arranged horizontally: Opportunity Score, Risk Score, and Final Score—each with progress ring visualizations. A Max Bid Calculator widget appears on the right side with input fields for estimated value, rehab costs, and desired profit margin. The Due Diligence Checklist section shows completed and pending items with checkboxes. A Zoning panel displays buildability data with setback requirements and lot coverage percentages.

**Caption:** "Complete due diligence in one view"

---

## Screenshot 4: Scoring Breakdown

**Title:** AI-Powered Investment Score Analysis

**Visual Description:**
Analytics-focused screen featuring a horizontal score distribution bar chart at the top, segmenting properties into emerald (strong), blue (good), amber (fair), and red (poor) categories with count labels on each segment. Below the chart, color-coded badges display detailed breakdowns with numerical scores and confidence intervals. A Data Confidence meter appears as a circular gauge showing the percentage of data fields verified for the selected property. A yellow micro-parcel alert banner warns when a property falls below minimum acreage thresholds for certain investment strategies.

**Caption:** "AI-powered investment scoring"

---

## Screenshot 5: Investor Report

**Title:** Professional Multi-Property Investment Report

**Visual Description:**
Print-optimized report layout in a clean multi-property format. The top section contains a Portfolio Overview with aggregate statistics: total properties selected, average opportunity score, total estimated investment required, and projected portfolio return. Below, per-property Investment Summary cards display in a two-column grid, each showing a property photo, address, key metrics (score, max bid, ARV estimate), and a brief rationale paragraph. The design uses clear section dividers and whitespace optimized for PDF export or committee presentation printing.

**Caption:** "Professional reports for committees"

---

## Screenshot 6: Dashboard

**Title:** Portfolio Intelligence Dashboard

**Visual Description:**
Dashboard overview screen with four stat cards across the top row showing: Total Properties Available, Properties Scored 80+, Average Opportunity Score, and Upcoming Sale Date. Below left, a Score Distribution donut chart visualizes the breakdown of property ratings. To the right, a Top Rated Bid-Targets list displays 5 properties with their scores and addresses in descending order. A Research Queue section at the bottom shows properties flagged for manual review with thumbnail images and quick-action buttons to open detail views.

**Caption:** "Portfolio intelligence at a glance"
