## Key Messages

### Problem
Tax sales require 10+ hours of manual research per property. Investors spend days gathering parcel data, zoning information, title records, and buildability metrics—time that costs deals and erodes margins.

### Solution
Automated analysis of 127 properties with AI-powered scoring. Our system evaluates every parcel across 15+ risk and value dimensions, delivering ranked opportunities with calculated maximum bids in minutes, not days.

### Differentiation
Not just listings—intelligence with calculated max bids. Every property receives a composite investment score, risk flags, and a data-driven maximum bid recommendation based on ARV, rehab estimates, and margin requirements.

---

## Value Propositions

### 1. Save Hours of Manual Research Per Property
Eliminate spreadsheet hunting across county assessor sites, GIS systems, zoning portals, and title databases. Our automated data aggregation reduces research time from hours to minutes.

### 2. Avoid Bad Investments with Data-Driven Scoring
Every property receives a composite risk score based on 15+ factors: micro-parcel detection, buildability constraints, zoning restrictions, ownership structure, and title clarity. Let the data filter out losers before you waste time.

### 3. Professional Reports for Investment Committees
Generate institutional-grade investment memos with property details, comparable sales, risk analysis, and recommended bids. Perfect for presenting to partners, lenders, or investment committees.

### 4. Real-Time Zoning and Buildability Data
Access current zoning classifications, setback requirements, and buildability calculations. Know exactly what you can build—and what you cannot—before you bid.

### 5. Risk Detection That Spreadsheets Miss
Automatic flagging of micro-parcels, entity-owned properties, clouded title indicators, and other deal-killers that manual research often overlooks.

## Target Audience

### Primary
Real estate investors, developers, and house flippers actively acquiring properties through tax sales. These users understand acquisition math, hold professional capital, and value speed and accuracy.

### Secondary
Investment committees, private lenders, and wholesalers who evaluate or finance tax sale acquisitions. Users who need documented analysis and risk assessment for due diligence processes.

### NOT For
Casual homebuyers, first-time buyers, or hobbyist investors seeking consumer-friendly property browsing. This is a professional tool for professional capital deployment.
