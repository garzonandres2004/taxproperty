# Workstream A: App Identity & Branding

## 1. App Name

**Recommendation: TaxProperty**

**Rationale:**

Keep "TaxProperty" and own it as the category leader. The name is concise, professional, and domain-specific. "TaxProperty Utah" limits expansion potential. "TaxProperty Intelligence" is redundant—intelligence is implied by the target user (institutional investors). Alternative: **"TaxProperty Pro"** if a modifier is needed to signal professional-grade tools.

---

## 2. Subtitle (30 characters max)

**Selected:** `Tax Sale Intelligence`

**Alternatives considered:**
- Tax Lien Analytics (20 chars)
- Utah Property Deals (19 chars)
- Investor Due Diligence (22 chars)

---

## 3. Tagline

> **"Know which properties to bid. Ignore the rest."**

**Why it works:**

Direct, binary framing mirrors the app's scoring system (Bid/Recommend/Avoid). Eliminates ambiguity. Signals decisiveness over discovery.

---

## 4. App Store Categories

| Priority | Category |
|----------|----------|
| **Primary** | Finance |
| **Secondary** | Business |

**Rationale:**

Avoid "Real Estate" category—it signals consumer home search (Zillow, Redfin). Tax sales are financial instruments first, real property second. Target users think like bond traders, not homebuyers.

---

## 5. Icon Concept

**Visual Direction:**

Abstract geometric mark, not a literal house. Monogram-style "TP" or single strong symbol (chevron, scale, or target reticle). Flat design, no gradients.

**Color Palette:**

- **Primary:** Deep navy (#0A1628) or charcoal (#1F2937)
- **Accent:** Signal amber (#F59E0B) or institutional gold—represents the scoring system
- **Background:** Dark mode default (professional terminal aesthetic)

**Metaphor:** Precision instrument. Think Bloomberg, not Zillow. The icon should look at home next to trading terminals and portfolio dashboards.

---

## 6. Primary Value Proposition

> **TaxProperty scores every property at Utah County tax sales so you bid with conviction, not guesswork.**

**Breakdown:**
- **What it does:** Scores properties (Bid/Recommend/Avoid)
- **Where:** Utah County tax sales (expandable geography)
- **For whom:** Serious investors requiring conviction
- **Differentiator:** Eliminates guesswork through systematic evaluation
